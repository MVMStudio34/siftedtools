The GEO Barometer measures which AI tools ChatGPT, Gemini and Perplexity actually recommend when asked about e-commerce.

## Why it matters

A growing share of tool discovery now starts with AI search. If an LLM doesn't mention your tool, you're invisible to that audience. The Barometer tracks this visibility monthly.

## How it works

We query 4 LLMs (GPT-4o, Gemini, Perplexity Sonar, Mistral) with real e-commerce questions across 10 categories. We measure which tools get cited, how often, and in what position. The score is calculated by [Quotis](https://quotis.ai), an independent measurement no vendor can buy or influence.
