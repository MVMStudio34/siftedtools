Every comparison on SiftedTools follows the same process. No exceptions.

## Real-store testing

We install each tool on live e-commerce stores. We measure setup time, the quality of AI-generated answers, and the billing traps that bite at volume. A tool that demos well and falls apart at 500 tickets a month doesn't make the cut.

## What we measure

Setup friction, AI answer accuracy, cost predictability at scale, integration depth with Shopify and WooCommerce, and how fast a new team member gets productive.

## The GEO Barometer

We track which tools the AI engines actually recommend, using [Quotis](https://quotis.ai), an independent AI-visibility score. It measures how often a tool gets surfaced by ChatGPT, Gemini and Perplexity. No vendor can buy or influence this signal.

## Editorial independence

Affiliate links fund the site. They never influence rankings. Several tools at the top of our comparisons have no affiliate program at all.
