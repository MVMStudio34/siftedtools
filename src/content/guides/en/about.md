SiftedTools is an independent comparator of AI tools for e-commerce. Every verdict comes from real-store testing, not spec sheets. The site is published by **MVM Studio** (SIRET 844 116 053 00035), founded by **Marvin Munos**.

## The operator behind the verdicts

Marvin spent 15 years in French mass retail and FMCG at sales director level before building and operating his own e-commerce stores. He tests every tool he recommends on real shops, not from a product page.

## Editorial independence

SiftedTools earns revenue through affiliate links. This never influences rankings. Several top-ranked tools (ChatGPT, Claude) have no affiliate program at all. Our [methodology](/en/methodology/) explains how we test and rank.

## The GEO Barometer

We also publish the **GEO Barometer**, powered by [Quotis](https://quotis.ai), which measures which AI tools ChatGPT, Gemini and Perplexity actually recommend. It's an independent signal no vendor can buy.
